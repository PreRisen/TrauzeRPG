The land of Melvor was once peaceful, but an ancient evil has plunged it into chaos. In a final act of desperation, the God of Faith resurrects a fallen warrior—you. But your past is lost, your memories erased.

You awaken in the ruins of an ancient castle, surrounded by a land frozen in endless winter. The north is on the brink of collapse, and only you can uncover why. But this is just the beginning—your journey will take you far beyond the snow, through kingdoms in turmoil, forgotten ruins, and lands untouched by time.

Will you bring peace back to Melvor, or will darkness consume it once more?
